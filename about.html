About me

Namasthe!

I love learning stuff and building things. I care deeply about making a difference in this world through sustainable, innovative technology. I love to travel. I come from a country with a rich, diverse culture and having traveled to multiple places, my perspective of looking at things has widened deeply. My international exposure has enriched my life with the ability to emphathise with distinct regions that are more greatly affected by global problems. I believe a successful engineer is shaped better by their bag of experiences and exposure to different cultures and their problems. A tragic medical incident in my family in my final undergradaute years gravitated me towards solving problems related to healthcare. Recently, I've been quite interested in trying to develop medical devices that have the potential to empower people to live a better life.
